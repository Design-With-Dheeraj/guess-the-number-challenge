Rules

  • Rule 1 - Click on the generate button which will generate a random number between 0 to 50 and that number will be shown only if your guess is correct.
  • Rule 2 - After clicking on the generate button, guess the number in the input field and click on the check button to see whether your guess is correct or not.
  • Rule 3 - Keep in mind that the check button will not work until you click on the generate button.
  • Rule 4 - You only have 10 chances.

    • Advantage

  • When you guess your number, you will get to see whether your number is greater or smaller than the random number. So that it will be easy for you to guess the number.
